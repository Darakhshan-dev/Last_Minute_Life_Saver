AI Task Prioritization

Gemini AI scores every task by deadline proximity, effort, and urgency — telling you exactly what to do first.

Google Calendar Sync

Connects to your real calendar, detects free windows, and schedules work around your actual commitments.

Push Notifications

Backend-powered FCM notifications reach your phone even when the app tab is fully closed.

Voice AI Chat

Daily Focus Plan

Every task gets a live risk badge — safe, at risk, high risk, or overdue — updated as time passes.
