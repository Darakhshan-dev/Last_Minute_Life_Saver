🔥 Last-Minute Life Saver

An AI-powered productivity companion that proactively helps students and professionals plan, prioritize, and complete tasks before deadlines are missed.

🎯 Purpose

Last-Minute Life Saver moves beyond traditional passive reminders. It uses Google Gemini AI to analyze your workload, sync with your Google Calendar, and send intelligent push notifications — even when your browser tab is closed — to help you take action before it's too late.

✨ Key Features

  • 🤖 AI-powered task prioritization using Google Gemini
  • 📅 Google Calendar integration with free/busy window detection
  • 🔔 Push notifications on desktop and Android phone (tab closed)
  • 🎙️ Voice-enabled AI chat assistant
  • 📊 Daily AI-generated focus plan
  • ⚡ Real-time deadline risk scoring

🛠️ Built With

React + TypeScript Google Gemini AI Firebase Google Calendar API FCM Push Notifications Node.js + Express
